# Privacy Policy for Unicode Guard

Unicode Guard does not collect, transmit, sell or share personal data.

The extension analyzes page text, links and domain names locally inside the user's browser to detect suspicious Unicode characters, homoglyphs, invisible characters and bidi controls.

Settings are stored using Chrome storage. Temporary bypass choices are stored locally and expire automatically.

Unicode Guard does not use remote code, external analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, cookies or external servers.
