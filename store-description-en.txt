Unicode Guard helps detect visually deceptive links and domains created with Unicode characters.

It detects many common homoglyph attack patterns: Cyrillic, Greek or Armenian characters that resemble Latin letters, invisible characters, bidi controls, mixed-script domains, suspicious punycode and risky Latin lookalikes that can imitate known brands.

The extension runs locally in the browser and does not send data to external servers.

Normal mode is designed for Latin-alphabet users. Greek detection is enabled by default for safer protection, but it can be disabled by users who regularly use legitimate Greek text or domains.
